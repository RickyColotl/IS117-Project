Company History

A Journey of Dedication and Growth

Colotl Construction LLC was born out of a dream, hard work, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Our founder, Gregory Colotl, began his journey in the construction industry over 20 years ago. His initial role was seemingly modest, responsible for maintaining clean and organized work sites. But even in this capacity, Gregory demonstrated an exceptional work ethic and a keen eye for detail.

Over the years, he advanced through the ranks, gaining invaluable hands-on experience in various facets of construction. His dedication and talent did not go unnoticed, and he swiftly moved up from basic tasks to more complex construction responsibilities. This period was instrumental in shaping his understanding of the industry, from foundational work to intricate finishing details.

In 2009, Gregory Colotl took a significant leap, channeling his years of experience and passion into founding Colotl Construction LLC. The company was built on the principles of integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction. Under his leadership, Colotl Construction has grown into a reputable and trusted name in the construction industry.

Today, Colotl Construction stands as a testament to the power of hard work and perseverance. We specialize in a range of services, including new home construction, renovations, and commercial projects. Our team, carefully selected and trained, mirrors the founder's commitment to excellence. We take pride in our ability to transform visions into reality, ensuring each project reflects our high standards of craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Our journey continues, driven by our dedication to building not just structures, but strong, lasting relationships with our clients. We look forward to bringing more dreams to life and furthering our legacy in the construction industry.

Our Mission

At Colotl Construction LLC, our mission is to deliver exceptional construction services that embody the highest standards of quality, efficiency, and sustainability. We are dedicated to:

  • Building Excellence

    By utilizing superior craftsmanship, the latest technology, and the finest materials, we aim to exceed expectations in every project we undertake, whether it's a residential home, commercial building, or renovation work.

  • Client-Centric Approach

    Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to understand their vision and needs, ensuring transparent communication and personalized service from concept to completion.

  • Safety and Compliance

    Maintaining a safe work environment for our team and clients is paramount. We adhere to strict safety protocols and compliance with all local building codes and regulations.

  • Sustainable Practices

    We are committed to incorporating eco-friendly practices and materials in our construction processes, contributing to a healthier environment and sustainable future.

  • Community and Employee Development

    As a company rooted in community values, we aim to positively impact our local communities through responsible business practices and by fostering the growth and development of our employees.


Through these commitments, Colotl Construction LLC aspires to be more than just a construction company – we aim to be a reliable partner in turning your construction dreams into enduring realities.

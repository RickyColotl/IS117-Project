FAQ's
  • Q: How do I start a project with Colotl Construction?

    A: Starting your project is easy! Just give us a call or fill out our online contact form. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project in detail, understand your needs, and outline the next steps.

  • Q: What kind of construction services do you offer?

    A: We offer a wide range of construction services including new home construction, renovations, extensions, kitchen and bathroom remodels, and commercial fit-outs. No project is too big or too small for us.

  • Q: Can you work within my budget?

    A: Absolutely. We work closely with our clients to understand their budget constraints and provide the best possible solutions within those parameters. We believe in transparency, so there will be no hidden costs.

  • Q: How long will my construction project take?

    A: The timeline for your project will depend on its scope and complexity. We'll provide a detailed schedule before we begin and keep you updated on our progress. Our team works diligently to meet deadlines while ensuring the highest quality.

  • Q: Do I need to obtain permits for my construction project?

    A: Yes, you would need to obtain the permits and we will handle the rest. This allows us to focus more on the project at hand.

  • Q: How do you ensure the quality of materials used?

    A: We source our materials from reputable suppliers and only use high-quality products. Our team carefully selects materials that meet our standards for durability, aesthetics, and sustainability.
